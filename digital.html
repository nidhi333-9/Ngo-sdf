Appraisal and Monitoring: An independent appraisal and monitoring teams (AMTs) is the heart of SDF’s quality assurance and fund disbursement mechanism who independently review community submissions for release of fund instalment. Appraisal and monitoring teams not only appraise community readiness to receive funds but also check compliance issues with on the basis of COM (Community Operations Manual) guidelines.
