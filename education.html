Education

    Free Tuition Support for Slum children:
The aim of this initiative is to provide supplementary education to children in rural and slum areas as well as to identify those children who are devoid of any form of education and enable them to achieve a certain level of literacy. For Slum Children, being financially challenged, quality education still remains elusive, irrespective of their geographical proximity to it. It is completely free of cost and any child who have will, can come and receive basic education. SDF is implementing the said program in JJ Cluster, Jahangirpuri, Delhi.
