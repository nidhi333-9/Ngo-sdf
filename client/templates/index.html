Overview

Sustainable Development Foundation is a civil society organization that has evolved its identity as autonomous non-profit organization registered under Indian Trust Registration Act and undertaken holistic development of the deprived and unprivileged and poor section of the society, which positively impact the lives of the poor and marginalized sections of the society by encouraging and enabled them to participate in the process of development through a people cantered approach. Over the past 6 years Sustainable Development Foundation contributing in the area of; Promotion Handicraft Items, Capacity Building, Social and Legal awareness, Natural Resource Management, sustainable Livelihood promotion, HIV/AIDS prevention among Injecting Drug users, Drinking water and sanitation, Female literacy, formal education for children and capacity building of the social sector organizations working at the grass root level through its professional staffs. The activities reach out to poor and marginalized, women, adolescents and disadvantaged from the rural urban areas with special focus on unserved and underserved backward areas of different parts of India. Sustainable Development Foundation believes in participatory approach and works accordingly for the effective and sustainable development. Ensures community participation at every stage of development process from the problem and need identification to project formulation, implementation, monitoring & evaluation. Sustainable Development Foundation firmly believes that the people’s participation leads to long-term efficiency, cost effectiveness, self-reliance, grass root initiatives and finally to enhance the community level organizational capacity. Since 5 Years Sustainable Development Foundation has entered with its innovative intervention which focus on Skill Upgrading of women groups, unemployed Youth groups at community on Micro Enterprise Establishment and various Income Generation program. And also organization felt that development without involvement of women’s is meaningless. Various stakeholders acknowledge Sustainable Development Foundation projects as model project and every year students from various premier social institutes and social workers from within the country and abroad make exposure visit to its project area to see the successful combination of advocacy and service delivery in the project intervention

Vision

Transforming lives of underprivileged and poor people of the Society through holistic interventions

Mission

Helping Society towards a search for self-reliance, socio economic upliftment and sustainable development

Goal

To create Sustainable Livelihood through impacting 500000 Lives by 2025

Our major Achievments since 2016

  • 2500 Blankets distributed to 2000 families staying in roadside/JJ colony/shelter home in East and South-West District of Delhi.
  • Street Childeren identified in South-West district of Delhi are linked with Government Schemes/Services.
  • 1000 Youths (both male & female) provided skill training on various trades i.e; Computer Application , Cutting & Tailoring , Beauty & Wellness, Mobile Repairing , Repairing of Electrical Home Appliances and Contruction Work in Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Haryana and Delhi.
  • 100 SHG Functionalized (formed, managed, Capacity Building & Bank linkages and 1000 women members are empowered.
  • 50 Women established their own micro enterprise after attending MEDP.
  • 1500 Farmers identified, trained and received exposure visit and now they are managing their own FPOs (Farmer Produced Organization)
  • 200 IEC program organized on various social issues (Nukkar-Natak, Rally, Quiz Competition in School).
